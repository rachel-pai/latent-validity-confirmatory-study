When may an assistant use something it remembers?

You will read 24 short situations. For each one, judge whether a highlighted remembered detail may be used in the reply. The pilot normally takes 25–35 minutes.

Your main judgment

  • Yes: the remembered detail may be used here.
  • No: the remembered detail should not be used here.

Judge only from the visible situation. Truth and relevance are separate questions: a detail can be true and relevant but still inappropriate to use.

Practice

Example

A verified booking says Room 7. The meeting organizer asks for the room number, and the booking notice says organizers may use it for scheduling.

Show explanation

Yes. The detail is current, relevant, and permitted for this person and purpose.

Example

A current payroll note is requested by the assigned analyst, but the request says it will be used to rank promotion candidates. The notice permits its use only to correct payroll.

Show explanation

No. Its truth does not make the proposed use appropriate.

Example

A request is signed Alex Kim, but the visible records contain two people with that name and do not identify which one sent it.
